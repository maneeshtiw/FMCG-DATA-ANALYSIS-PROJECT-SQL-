# FMCG-DATA-ANALYSIS-PROJECT-SQL-
End-to-end FMCG revenue and sales analysis to uncover business insights and trends.

I worked on an FMCG revenue dataset to analyze sales performance and identify business improvement opportunities.

Using SQL, I first cleaned and transformed the data, then performed exploratory analysis on revenue, profit, and regional performance. I used aggregations, joins, and window functions to identify trends like month-over-month growth, top-performing products, and profit margins across regions.

One key insight I found was that a particular region was generating the highest revenue but had significantly lower profit margins compared to others. After deeper analysis, I identified that higher operational costs and heavy discounting were the main reasons behind this.

Based on this, I recommended optimizing the supply chain and revisiting pricing strategies in that region to improve profitability. I also identified that a small percentage of products were contributing to the majority of revenue, so I suggested focusing marketing efforts on those high-performing products.

Overall, this project helped me strengthen my ability to not just write SQL queries, but also derive actionable business insights from data.
